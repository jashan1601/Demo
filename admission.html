Admissions

A. D. Patel Institute of Technology strictly follows the rules and guidelines given by AICTE, Government of Gujarat for admissions in Under Graduate and Post Graduate programs.

Students willing to secure admission under management quota or under NRI/Sponsored quota are required to contact college administration after 12th Board examination.

Admission Inquiry Office is open from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. (Week Days), 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM(Saturday) & 10:00 AM to 12:00 Noon (Sunday)
